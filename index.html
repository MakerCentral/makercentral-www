Welcome to Maker Central, a freely accessible "maker-space" that lets you explore your interests and turn your passions into reality! This space is equipped with amazing tools and resources needed for your design, fabrication and electronics projects.

Capabilities

Maker Central features a wide variety of equipment including laser cutters, 3D printers, vinyl cutters, and more.

Have questions?

We're here to answer them.

Why was Maker Central created?

Maker Central was created so anyone could bring their ideas to life without restrictions.

Who can use Maker Central?

Anyone can use Maker Central. That means you! You must complete the Central Spark Orientation tour starting just outside Central Spark at noon on the last Thursday of the month in order to use Maker Central. You don't need an appointment to take the tour.

What do you do in Maker Central?

You can use a variety of the capabilities and resources available in Maker Central to create whatever you would like.

When can I use Maker Central?

Maker Central is open 24/7. Come in anytime!

Where is Maker Central?

Maker Central is located within Central Spark in Building 17.

How do I join?

Stop by Maker Central to start working on a project. You could also submit information to be featured on the People and/or Projects pages!
