Microelectronics Classes

Microprocessor 101

Learn the basics of design and development using microcontrollers, sensors and mechatronics. Course covers the basic setup and use of the Arduino Uno and the Arduino IDE. A hands on course for students of all skill levels.

Sign up for this class Teach this class

Microprocessor 201

Prerequisites: Microprocessor 101
Continue your studies of microprocessor design and development continuing into ARM based microprocessor development. Course covers the Raspberry Pi and the various interfaces it can use to talk to the outside world.

Microprocessor 301

Prerequisites: Microprocessor 101 and 201
Continue your studies of microprocessor design and development. Leave the development kits behind and program your own bare microcontrollers so that you can directly incorporate them into your customer circuits.
